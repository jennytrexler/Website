Mission Statement
What we Believe

The mission of the Riverview Soccer Club is to provide a competitive but fun soccer experience for the soccer player. We as a club will create an environment that will provide each player a valuable learning experience, an opportunity to improve their skills, build character and provide an encouraging environment where our youth can make friendships that can last a lifetime. Riverview Soccer Club will also strive to build self-esteem, self-confidence and team success. This will be done through hard work, commitment, exercise and fun.
